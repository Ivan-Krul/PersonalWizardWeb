Terms and Details

To contact me you can choose the related tab above. To get a price of request, you can look at preferences, at which configuration I would draw down below. There is an additional mode to choose precisely which configuration you want and copy and paste the request when you contacting me (I will understand what it says). You choose at which option you can pay, you can pay immediately, or you can pay partially, 50% in line art, 50% at rest of the drawing. In case you are lacking of money or you have no access to payment systems, you can contribute to my projects, or you can draw one of my characters, the main thing, this has to be generous and good quality.
You can ask for the refund with full prise at line art stage and 50% at the rest of the stage. If I did not not finish your request in the span of week, you would have an opportunity to request another drawing. At the finish of drawing let me know would you post it somewhere and would you ping me in social medias.

I draw stuff in simplified style, keeping detailing in low. I preferably draw humanoids or furries. I would not make any animations. I can draw landscapes, simple machines or some abstraction. There is many things I can draw in basic level.
I would not accept request that I can not creatively express in drawing. I will not draw any suggestive things or many blood, even if it is for the sake of money.
